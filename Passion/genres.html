Genres: A World of Variety

From action-packed blockbusters to intimate dramas, cinema offers a variety of genres to suit all tastes. Horror, comedy, romance, and science fiction are just a few examples of how filmmakers explore different themes and emotions. Each genre has its own style, yet all share the common goal of storytelling.
