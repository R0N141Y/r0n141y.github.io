The Evolution of Cinema

Cinema, one of the most influential forms of art, has evolved tremendously since its inception. From silent films to the introduction of sound, color, and digital effects, each era of filmmaking has shaped the way we experience stories on screen. Today, cinema continues to push boundaries, blending visual effects with captivating narratives.
